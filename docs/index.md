### What is TubeBay?

TubeBay is a powerful, lightweight WordPress plugin designed specifically for WooCommerce store owners who want to leverage the power of video to increase conversions. By bridging the gap between your YouTube library and your product catalog, TubeBay allows you to seamlessly sync, manage, and display product-specific videos without the manual overhead of copying and pasting embed codes.

### Why Video Matters for WooCommerce?

In modern e-commerce, video is no longer optional. It builds trust, clarifies product features, and significantly increases time-on-page.
- **Boost Conversions:** Seeing a product in action helps customers make informed decisions faster.
- **Better SEO:** Video content improves dwell time, which is a key ranking factor for search engines.
- **Professionalism:** High-quality YouTube embeds make your store feel premium and up-to-date.

### Core Workflow

1. **Connect:** Authenticate with YouTube via Google OAuth or a manual API key.
2. **Sync:** Your latest videos are automatically pulled into your WordPress dashboard.
3. **Map:** Quickly search and link videos to your WooCommerce products.
4. **Display:** Videos appear automatically on your product pages in your preferred location.
